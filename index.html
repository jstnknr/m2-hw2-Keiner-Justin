Justin Keiner

Biography

Youth

Justin was born in Mississippi, but did not live there long as his father was in the navy. The Keiner family moved throughout the South East, eventually settling in Pensacola for 5 years. They then moved to Columbia, South Carolina, where they currently reside. Justin graduated from Blythewood High School.

Education

Justin started his higher education journey at Winthrop University in Rockhill, South Carolina. While he was there he was pursuing a degree in Art Education. He would transfer to the Unversity of South Carolina the following year. He graduated in May 2020 during the Covid-19 Pandemic.

Career

Justin is currently a teacher of the Visual Arts at Spring Valley High School in Columbia, South Carolina. He teachs a variety of classes including photography and media arts. Justin work at a local seafood restuarant on the weekends.
